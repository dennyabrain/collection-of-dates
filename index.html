A collection of some consequential and inconsequential dates

26th December 2016

I feel slightly bothered by the fact that you did not wish me on Christmas.


29th December 2016

I begin thinking that you never really were a Chrismas wisher, anyway. So maybe I should not be that upset.


1st January 2017

I realize that I forgot to wish you on your birthday and so maybe I should not be upset AT ALL!


17th December 2016

I go to an art exhibition on digital surveillance and see some 3D-like postcards. I feel that I might be wrong and I am probably wrong but they look like something that a strange being like you may cherish. You probably would just go meh and toss them amongst a pile of nothings. Or possibly you would totally go berserk about how cool they are and send it to loved ones. There is a small chance that this is possibly one of the best things I could give you. Since that is a rare occasion I am willing to tell the convinience seeking side of my brain to lay low and make plans of posting these to you.


2nd July 2013

You tell me you like postcards.


17th August 2015

You post a letter from Berkley to me hence revealing your address.


10th November 2014

The date I thought I would send you a bunch of gifts but the logistics of international postage seemed like the valid excuse (back then) for me to not send you anything.


9th October 2015

I decided I should definitely send you something but what could I send that is not generic enough to feel meaningless and also not meaningful enough to feel like a subtle sublimal message


27th Decelber 2016

I watch a really good movie La La Land and think of you. I wanted to recommend you to watch it but am too taken over by my ego because of the fact that you did not wish me on Christmas


6th December 2016

You post your images of goodle timelapse on your blog that makes a light go off in my dumb head. Even though you are a nerd of many things, and since I don't share a lot of those passions with you, it is hard to place a finger on what physical object you might like. But is it possible that a LOTR reading, Ursula Le Guin loving being might be into Maps?


17th December 2016

I ask the people at the art exhibition how much the postcards cost and then tell me they are for free. I fee a little sad because I really wanted to spend money on them. Because they felt like they would be worth it. I was willing to spend around 20 dollars for two postcard because I felt if you liked them they would be nice for you to keep one with yourself and send another one to a loved one. Since they were free I picked up all unique copies and also some not so good postcards coz I am sure you would want to send postcards to your not-that-much-loved-ones too


2nd January 2017

I decide to post these lovely postcards to you in an envelope because I am too worried that if I post them actually as postcards they will get stolen coz they look so good. I had to change plans because the post office was closed due to them observing an extended new year.


2nd January 2017

This was not planned at all but you happen to ping me and we talk on phone after so long. Throughout the call I am worried that your birthday will come up. When it does, I literally choke myself to prevent me from saying that 'Actually I was planning a surprise for you' thereby showcasing some growth and character as a young man


1st January 2017

I finally figured out what the letter accompanying the postcards should be like.


Happy Belated Birthday! I hope you like these. Looking forward to hearing more stories from you at some point when our paths cross.
